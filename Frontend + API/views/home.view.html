Revolutionising collaborative donations

Bridging the gap between donors, ambassadors and beneficiers

We want to be the innovative hub of collaborative donation-platforms to connect, en­gage and em­power donors and re­ceiving organisations (ambassadors), ranging from United Nations to individual donors, with the network and know­ledge of what is needed at any given time, to help refugees in health­care, edu­cation, integration, child protection and women em­power­ment. We want to to provide sustainable and responsible solutions and a better future.

To achieve this mission, Codonation envisions to work together with responsible, reliable and precious partners and networks.

How it Works

Donors

Step 1

Register Donations

Register your donations; items, amount and location. Don’t worry, only veri­fied or­gani­sations (am­bassa­dors) can view, request and collect your donated items. Give the best de­scription so others will know about your donations.

Step 2

Collaborate

Reach out to donors and ambassa­dors. No one knows the subject better than you, so empower this plat­form to excel and help the ones in need. Pave them the ways, and un­leash the powers of col­laborative platforms.

Step 3

Get Ratings

Don’t forget to get your am­bassadors to rate you! Higher rated donors will ap­pear first on the do­nations' list. You know what they say: The best place to hide a dead body is on page two.

Ambassadors

Step 1

Get verified

Apply to become an Ambassa­dor organisation to deliver the donated items from our donors' platform directly to refugees in need.

Step 2

Connect

Connect with the selected do­nors and request the available donations. You can enrol in multiple requests, but don’t bite more than you can chew! Collect & Deliver

Step 3

Do

Do what you must to be the best: collect & deliver to ones in need on time. Make the gifts for better lives.

How can you help?

Codonation is focusing on the Syrian refugee crisis. Through our platform we are connecting a spectrum of donors and verified organisations to deliver items from bandages, blankets, first aid kits and toys to refugees in need.
